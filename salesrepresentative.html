My old days In Mercuries Life Insurance

My role in a well-known life insurance company as a sales representative in Taiwan was the first and foremost experience that built up my passion for marketing. During that time, I helped clients find the best products based on their budget, career risks level, life expectations, etc. With the support from my supervisors and the opportunities given by clients, I had won multiple prizes that showed my ability and value toward the company.

At the beginning of doing this job, I'd encountered various challenges that made me frustrated. For example, people in my country usually share a negative impression of a salesperson, so it took me a lot of effort and time to adapt to this situation.

So, "what was the biggest achievement I had made in this job?" if you asked. I'd say that because most of the clients that acquired were strangers to me, they weren't as friendly as I expected when I tried to approach them in the first place. However, we ended up in a trusted relationship. The bonding was the achievement and the reward that made me grow the most.
