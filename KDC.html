Why KDC?

I love dancing! And I'd danced for 10+ years. Due to my passion for it, I decided to start a service for people who would like to learn street dance with my friends I knew in university.

What I did?

At first, I was excited and did everything myself, from finding a good photographer, building a Facebook fan page, creating and editing videos and posts and sharing across relevant Facebook groups, learning how to advertise on Facebook, etc.

Two weeks later or so, our FB page reached about 400 followers without running FB ads, and our busiest time was 6 classes per week.

What challenges I had?

However, it wasn't easy to manage this business for a few reasons. Firstly, most teachers (my friends) in KDC all work full-time jobs, which caused uncertainty when matching the schedule with the customers'. Secondly, we didn't have a website to drive organic traffic. As a result, we lost opportunities to build brand awareness and convert the leads. Lastly, there were not enough trustworthy teachers to work with and answer course-related questions from parents.

What would I do if I could start again?

After a comprehensive review of KDC, If I would started the business over again, I would have created a website and recruited qualified teachers.
